# Welcome Sequence Template (7 emails, 14 days)

**Trigger**: New signup / account created
**Sender**: From a real person on your team (e.g., "Jamie from [Product]")
**Goal**: Orient → Activate → Build trust → Soft convert

---

## Email 1 — Welcome (Day 0, immediate)

**Subject lines**:
- A: "You're in — here's what happens next"
- B: "Welcome to [Product] — your setup guide"
- C: "{{first_name}}, let's get you started"

**Preview text**: "3 things to do in your first 10 minutes."

**Body**:
```
Hey {{first_name}},

Welcome — really glad you're here.

In the next few minutes, I want to make sure you know exactly where to start so you don't waste time clicking around. Here's what I'd suggest:

→ Step 1: [First key action — e.g., "Complete your brand profile"]
→ Step 2: [Second key action — e.g., "Run your first skill"]
→ Step 3: [Third key action — e.g., "Check out the templates library"]

If you hit any snags, reply to this email — I read every response.

[CTA Button: "Get started →"]

Talk soon,
[Name]
[Title], [Product]

P.S. Our quick-start guide is here if you prefer to read ahead: [link]
```

```
[Name]
```
