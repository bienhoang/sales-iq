# Feature Announcement Email Template

**Use for**: New feature releases, significant updates, major improvements
**Audience**: Active users first, then trial users, then churned (separate messaging)
**Goal**: Drive awareness → adoption of new feature

---

## Single Announcement Email

**Subject lines**:
- A: "New: [Feature Name] is live"
- B: "[Old way] → [New way] — [Feature Name] is here"
- C: "{{first_name}}, we just shipped something you'll use every day"

**Preview text**: "[One-line benefit statement — what it does FOR them]"

**Body**:
```
Hey {{first_name}},

[Feature Name] is live — and I think you're going to use it a lot.

**Before**: [Describe the painful old way — what did they have to do manually or inefficiently?]
**Now**: [Describe the new way — how does the feature change that experience?]

[Screenshot or GIF placeholder — show, don't just tell]

**How it works:**
1. [Step 1 — simple, action-oriented]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Result — the payoff]

[Early adopter quote or usage stat if available:
"[Quote from beta user about the feature]" — [Name], [Role] at [Company]]

[CTA Button: "Try [Feature Name] →"]

As always, reply here if you have questions or feedback.

[Name]
[Title], [Product]

P.S. [Optional — link to docs, tutorial video, or changelog]
```

---

## Follow-Up Tutorial Email (Day 3 after announcement)

**Subject lines**:
- A: "How to get the most out of [Feature Name]"
- B: "{{first_name}}, here's the [Feature Name] playbook"
- C: "3 ways to use [Feature Name] this week"

**Preview text**: "Most users start with [use case 1]."

**Body**:
```
Hey {{first_name}},

A few days ago I told you about [Feature Name]. Since then, [X] users have tried it — here's what they're doing with it:

**Use case 1: [Scenario]**
[2-3 sentences describing a real workflow. Who benefits, what they do, what result they get.]

**Use case 2: [Scenario]**
[2-3 sentences.]

**Use case 3: [Scenario]**
[2-3 sentences.]

Haven't tried it yet? Takes less than 2 minutes to set up.

[CTA Button: "Open [Feature Name] →"]

[Name]
```

---

## Segmentation Notes

| Segment | Angle | Subject Line Adjustment |
|:--------|:------|:------------------------|
| Active users | "New capability you can use today" | Lead with the feature benefit |
| Trial users | "Another reason to upgrade" | Tie to paid plan if feature is paid |
| Churned users | "Things have changed since you left" | Frame as "what's new" re-engagement |
| Power users | "Early access / beta" | Exclusivity and early feedback ask |

## Timing Recommendations
- **Send day**: Tuesday or Wednesday
- **Send time**: 9-10 AM recipient's local time
- **Follow-up**: 3 days after announcement (tutorial email)
- **Segment order**: Power users (T-2) → Active users (T-0) → Trial users (T+1)
