# Content Pillars Reference

## The 5 Pillars

### Pillar 1: Product Education (25%)

**Purpose**: Help existing and potential users understand your product's value.

**Content ideas**:
- "How to [do X] in [Product]" step-by-step
- Feature spotlight: "[Feature] explained in 60 seconds"
- "Did you know [Product] can do X?" tip posts
- Before/after: "Old way vs [Product] way"
- Use case: "How [role] uses [Product] to [outcome]"
- Template or checklist shares

**Best formats**:
- LinkedIn: Carousel PDF, text post with numbered steps
- Instagram: Carousel (step-by-step), Reel demo
- X/Twitter: Thread or single tweet with screenshot
- TikTok: Screen recording demo with voiceover

---

### Pillar 2: Thought Leadership (20%)

**Purpose**: Build authority, attract ideal customers through shared worldview.

**Content ideas**:
- Hot take on an industry trend or debate
- Data-backed insight: "We analyzed X — here's what we found"
- Contrarian opinion: "Why [common belief] is wrong"
- Prediction: "The future of [category] in [year]"
- Frameworks: "The [X]-step framework for [outcome]"
- Industry news commentary with original perspective

**Best formats**:
- LinkedIn: Long-form text post (500-1,500 chars), poll
- X/Twitter: Thread (5-10 tweets) or single hot-take tweet
- Instagram: Quote graphic + caption
- TikTok: Talking-head opinion video

---

### Pillar 3: Social Proof (20%)

**Purpose**: Build trust through real customer outcomes.

**Content ideas**:
- Customer quote or testimonial (with permission)
- Case study excerpt: "[Company] grew [metric] by [X]% with [Product]"
- Product milestone: "[X] teams now use [Product]"
- Review highlight from G2/Capterra
- User-generated content reshare (tag + celebrate)
- "Why [customer] switched from [competitor]"

**Best formats**:
- LinkedIn: Quote graphic or text post with story
- Instagram: Carousel (before/after or customer journey)
- X/Twitter: Quote tweet of customer praise + commentary
- TikTok: Customer testimonial video (with permission) or reading a great review

---

### Pillar 4: Culture & Team (15%)

**Purpose**: Humanize the brand, attract talent, build emotional connection.

**Content ideas**:
- Team introductions: "Meet [Name], our [Role]"
- Behind the scenes: How we built [feature]
- Company milestone: "[X] years, [X] customers, [X] team members"
- Values in action: "Why we decided to [value-driven decision]"
- Hiring announcements with team culture angle
- "A day in the life of a [role] at [Company]"
- Team wins and celebrations

**Best formats**:
- LinkedIn: Text post with team photo or candid moment
- Instagram: Photo or Reel (authentic, unpolished is OK)
- TikTok: Behind-the-scenes or "day in the life" video

---

### Pillar 5: Community (20%)

**Purpose**: Foster engagement, gather feedback, make audience feel part of something.

**Content ideas**:
- Poll: "Which do you prefer: A or B?"
- Question prompt: "What's your biggest [challenge] right now?"
- Challenge: "Try [action] this week and reply with your results"
- UGC prompt: "Show us how you use [Product]!"
- AMA announcement: "Ask [person] anything about [topic]"
- Community shoutout: "Shoutout to [member] for [contribution]"
- Discussion starter: "Hot take: [statement] — agree or disagree?"

**Best formats**:
- LinkedIn: Poll, question post
- Instagram: Question sticker in Stories, poll sticker
- X/Twitter: Poll, open-ended question
- TikTok: Duet or stitch prompt, challenge

## Seasonal Themes

Plan content themes around natural calendar moments:

| Month | Natural Themes | SaaS-Relevant Angles |
|:------|:--------------|:--------------------|
| Jan | New year, fresh start | "Plan your [year] marketing strategy" |
| Feb | Q1 check-in | "Are you on track for Q1?" |
| Mar | Spring planning | "Spring clean your [tool/process]" |
| Apr | Tax season over, Q2 | "Q2 marketing playbook" |
| May | Mid-year momentum | "Halfway there — marketing audit" |
| Jun | Q2 close | "Q2 recap + H2 preview" |
| Jul | Summer slowdown | Evergreen educational content |
| Aug | Back to business | "Back-to-business marketing toolkit" |
| Sep | Q3 push | "Q4 prep starts now" |
| Oct | Q4 planning | "Holiday campaign planning" |
| Nov | Black Friday/holiday | B2B: budget season; B2C: promo season |
| Dec | Year in review | "2025 in numbers + 2026 predictions" |
