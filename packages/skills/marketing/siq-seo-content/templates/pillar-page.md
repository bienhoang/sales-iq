# Pillar Page Template

Pillar pages are comprehensive, authoritative guides on a broad topic. They rank for high-volume head terms and link out to supporting cluster content.

**Target length**: 3,000–5,000 words
**Update frequency**: Every 6-12 months

---

## Content Brief

| Field | Value |
|:------|:------|
| **Title** | "The Complete Guide to [Topic]" or "Everything You Need to Know About [Topic]" |
| **Target Persona** | [ICP name] |
| **Funnel Stage** | TOFU (primarily) |
| **Primary Keyword** | [broad head term — high volume, high difficulty] |
| **Cluster Keywords** | [supporting blog post keywords this page links to] |
| **Word Count Target** | 3,000–5,000 words |
| **Content Goal** | Organic authority + cluster hub |
| **Internal Links Out** | [List 5-10 cluster blog posts this page will link to] |

## SEO Metadata

```
Title Tag: [Under 60 chars — "The [Year] Guide to [Keyword]"]
Meta Description: [Under 160 chars — promise comprehensiveness + value]
URL Slug: /blog/[broad-keyword-guide] or /guides/[topic]
```

## Page Structure

### H1: [Title — The Complete Guide to {Primary Keyword}]

**Introduction** (200-300 words):
- What this guide covers and who it's for
- Why this topic matters now (market context)
- What the reader will be able to do after reading
- Include primary keyword within first 100 words

---

### H2: What Is [Topic]? (Definition anchor)

**Purpose**: Capture "what is X" informational queries. Keep under 300 words.

- Clear, jargon-free definition
- Why it matters (business impact)
- Brief history or evolution (optional, if relevant)

---

### H2: Why [Topic] Matters for [Target Audience]

**Purpose**: Establish relevance and urgency.

- 3-5 reasons with data points
- Cost of ignoring this topic
- Who benefits most

---

### H2: [Core Concept 1] (repeat for 4-6 core concepts)

**Purpose**: Each H2 targets a secondary keyword and links to a cluster blog post.

[300-500 words covering this aspect thoroughly]

**→ Go deeper**: [Link to cluster blog post on this specific topic]

---

### H2: Step-by-Step: How to [Achieve Main Outcome]

**Purpose**: Highly practical section — often earns featured snippets.

1. **Step 1: [Action]** — [Explanation]
2. **Step 2: [Action]** — [Explanation]
3. **Step 3: [Action]** — [Explanation]
4. **Step 4: [Action]** — [Explanation]
