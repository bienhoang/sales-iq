# Keyword Research Guide

## Keyword Research Process

### Step 1: Seed Keywords
Start with 5-10 broad terms that describe your product category, core problems you solve, and target outcomes.

Examples for a SaaS marketing product:
- "saas marketing tools"
- "content marketing automation"
- "brand strategy software"
- "marketing workflow"

### Step 2: Expand with Tools

| Tool | Best For | Cost |
|:-----|:---------|:-----|
| Google Search Console | Your existing rankings, CTR, impressions | Free |
| Google Keyword Planner | Volume estimates, CPC data | Free (needs Ads account) |
| Ahrefs / Semrush | Competitor keywords, difficulty scores | Paid |
| Ubersuggest | Budget-friendly keyword research | Freemium |
| AnswerThePublic | Question-based keywords | Freemium |
| Google autocomplete | Real search queries, zero cost | Free |
| "People Also Ask" | FAQ and featured snippet opportunities | Free |

### Step 3: Classify Intent

| Intent Type | Signal Words | Best Content Format |
|:------------|:------------|:--------------------|
| **Informational** | what, how, why, guide, tutorial | Blog post, pillar page |
| **Navigational** | [brand] login, [brand] pricing | Landing page, docs |
| **Commercial** | best, top, alternatives, vs, review | Comparison page, listicle |
| **Transactional** | buy, pricing, free trial, sign up | Pricing page, landing page |

### Step 4: Evaluate Keywords

Score each keyword on:

| Factor | What to Look For |
|:-------|:----------------|
| **Search volume** | 100-10K/month sweet spot for new sites; high volume = high competition |
| **Keyword difficulty (KD)** | Target KD 0-40 when starting; 40-70 as you build authority |
| **Business relevance** | Will this traffic convert to trials/customers? |
| **SERP features** | Featured snippets, PAA boxes = opportunities to own |
| **Current ranking** | Already ranking 4-20? Optimize existing page first |

## Keyword Cluster Framework

Group keywords into topic clusters. Each cluster = one pillar page + multiple supporting blog posts.

### Example Cluster: "SaaS Content Marketing"

**Pillar page**: "The Complete Guide to SaaS Content Marketing"
- Primary keyword: "saas content marketing" (5,400/mo, KD 52)

**Supporting blog posts**:
- "How to build a SaaS content calendar" (720/mo, KD 28)
- "SaaS blog post templates that drive organic traffic" (390/mo, KD 22)
- "Content marketing metrics for SaaS companies" (480/mo, KD 31)
- "SaaS content marketing strategy for early-stage startups" (260/mo, KD 19)

**Comparison pages**:
- "[Product] vs Contently" (commercial intent, BOFU)
- "Best SaaS content marketing tools" (commercial intent, MOFU)

## Priority Matrix

| Priority | Criteria | Action |
|:---------|:---------|:-------|
| **P1 — Quick wins** | Ranking 4-20, high volume, high relevance | Optimize existing page |
| **P2 — High value** | KD <40, volume >500, high relevance | Create new content |
| **P3 — Long tail** | KD <20, volume 50-500, specific intent | Blog post or FAQ |
| **P4 — Competitive** | KD >60, high volume | Wait until domain authority grows |

## Competitive Keyword Analysis

For each top competitor, identify:
1. **Top pages by traffic** — what content drives their organic growth?
2. **Keyword gaps** — terms they rank for that you don't
3. **Content gaps** — topics they cover that you haven't addressed
4. **Featured snippets** — which answers are they owning that you could take?

### Gap Analysis Template

| Competitor | Their Top Keyword | Their Ranking | Your Ranking | Gap Opportunity |
|:-----------|:-----------------|:--------------|:-------------|:----------------|
| [Competitor A] | [keyword] | #[X] | Not ranking | Create content targeting this |
| [Competitor B] | [keyword] | #[X] | #[Y] | Improve existing page |
