## Algorithm Signals (2025-2026)

| Platform | Prioritizes | Deprioritizes |
|:---------|:-----------|:--------------|
| **LinkedIn** | Dwell time, comments, reshares with commentary | Link posts (lower organic reach), engagement bait |
| **X / Twitter** | Replies, quote tweets, bookmarks | Link-heavy tweets (bury the link in replies) |
| **Instagram** | Saves, shares to Stories, Reels watch time | Follows, likes (lower weight than before) |
| **TikTok** | Watch time %, completion rate, shares | Low production on non-trending topics |
| **Facebook** | Video watch time, group activity, comments | Organic reach for pages is low — video helps |

## Content Lifespan

| Platform | Organic Content Lifespan | Best Repurpose Window |
|:---------|:------------------------|:---------------------|
| LinkedIn | 24-72 hours (peak at 2-4hrs) | Repurpose to blog within 1 week |
| X / Twitter | 15-30 minutes (peak) | Retweet/repost evergreen threads monthly |
| Instagram | 24-48 hours (feed), 24hrs (stories) | Reuse carousels 3-6 months later |
| TikTok | Days to weeks (algorithm re-serves) | Old videos can resurface — evergreen wins |
| Facebook | 2-5 hours (organic pages) | Boost high-performers with small budget |
