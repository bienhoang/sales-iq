# Hashtag Strategy

## Platform-by-Platform Guidance

### LinkedIn
- **Recommended count**: 3-5 hashtags
- **Placement**: End of post or naturally within text
- **Strategy**: Mix one broad industry tag + one specific topic tag + one branded tag
- **Research**: Follow hashtags to see volume; LinkedIn shows "X followers" per hashtag

**Hashtag tiers for SaaS**:
| Type | Examples | When to Use |
|:-----|:---------|:------------|
| Broad (1M+ followers) | #Marketing, #SaaS, #Startup | Max 1 per post |
| Mid (100K-1M) | #ContentMarketing, #GrowthMarketing, #B2BMarketing | 1-2 per post |
| Niche (10K-100K) | #SaaSMarketing, #ProductLedGrowth, #MarketingOps | 1-2 per post |
| Branded | #[YourProduct], #[YourProductTip] | Always include |

### Instagram
- **Recommended count**: 15-20 (30 is allowed but 20 performs better)
- **Placement**: First comment (keeps caption clean) or end of caption after line breaks
- **Strategy**: Mix broad, mid-tier, niche, and branded

**Hashtag mix formula for Instagram**:
```
3-4 broad tags (500K-2M posts)
4-5 mid-tier tags (100K-500K posts)
5-6 niche tags (10K-100K posts)
2-3 community tags (your audience uses these)
1-2 branded tags
```

**Finding good hashtags**:
- Search Instagram → see "Top posts" — can you compete with those?
- Look at what competitors and industry leaders use
- Check "Related hashtags" suggestions

### X / Twitter
- **Recommended count**: 1-2 hashtags maximum
- **Placement**: Naturally within tweet text where possible
- **Strategy**: Only use hashtags if they add context or you're joining a conversation (e.g., conference hashtag)
- **Note**: Hashtags on X are less impactful than on Instagram — prioritize keywords over tags

### TikTok
- **Recommended count**: 3-5 hashtags
- **Placement**: In the description (150 char limit total including hashtags)
- **Strategy**: Mix trending + niche + branded. Check "Discover" tab for trending tags.
- **Always include**: #fyp or #foryoupage (may boost distribution) + 1-2 niche tags

### Facebook
- **Recommended count**: 1-3 hashtags (low value on Facebook)
- **Note**: Facebook hashtags have minimal algorithm impact — only use for cross-platform consistency or groups

---

## Branded Hashtag Guidelines

Create and consistently use:
- **Brand hashtag**: `#[YourProduct]` — use on every post
- **Content series hashtag**: `#[YourProduct]Tip`, `#[YourProduct]Weekly` — for recurring content
- **Community hashtag**: `#[YourCommunityName]` — encourage users to tag their work

**Rules for branded hashtags**:
- Check availability before launching (search to confirm no conflicting use)
- Include in bio on all platforms
- Feature in onboarding emails to encourage UGC
- Monitor for UGC and engage with it

---

## Hashtag Research Process

1. **Start with competitors**: What are your top 3 competitors using?
2. **Check industry leaders**: What do thought leaders in your space tag?
3. **Validate volume**: Too small = no reach; too large = buried immediately
4. **Test and track**: Rotate hashtag sets every 4-6 weeks and compare reach

**Sweet spot by platform**:
- Instagram: 10K-500K posts per hashtag
- LinkedIn: 50K-500K followers per hashtag
- TikTok: Any size if the content matches the community
