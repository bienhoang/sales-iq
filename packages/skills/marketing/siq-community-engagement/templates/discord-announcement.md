|:--------|:-------|:------------|

| > Quote | `> text` | Callouts, links, highlights |

| User mention | `<@user-id>` | Ping specific person (use sparingly) |
| Role mention | `<@&role-id>` | Ping a role group (use sparingly) |
| Embed color hint | In bot embeds | Green=success, Blue=info, Yellow=warning, Red=alert |
