# Research & Analysis Phase

**When to skip:** If provided with researcher reports, skip this phase.

## Core Activities

### Market & Strategy Research
- Use `siq-research` skill to investigate market trends, competitor strategies, and channel opportunities
- Assign focused research topics (e.g., channel benchmarks, ICP validation, pricing analysis)
- Wait for research findings before proceeding

### Sequential Thinking
- Use `siq-sequential-thinking` skill for dynamic and reflective problem-solving
- Structured thinking process for complex strategy analysis
- Enables multi-step reasoning with revision capability

### Documentation Research
- Use `siq-docs-seeker` skill to find documentation for marketing platforms, CRM APIs, ad networks
- Research tool capabilities, integration options, and platform constraints
- Find latest best practices for sales/marketing tools

### Strategic Framework Analysis
- Use `siq-strategy-consultant` skill for framework-based analysis when applicable:
  - Porter's Five Forces for market entry planning
  - SWOT for campaign/product positioning
  - Blue Ocean for differentiation strategies
  - TAM/SAM/SOM for market sizing

## Best Practices

- Research breadth before depth
- Document findings for synthesis phase
- Identify multiple approaches for comparison
- Consider market risks and competitor responses during research
- Note budget implications and ROI projections early
